Privacy & Cookies

GW Plastering & Rendering · gwplastering.co.uk

This page explains, in plain terms, what information GW Plastering & Rendering collects when you use this website or get in touch, and how cookies are used.

Who we are

GW Plastering & Rendering is a plastering and rendering business covering Exeter and Devon. You can reach us on 07557 355 420 or at gwplasteringdevon@gmail.com.

Information we collect

We only hold the information you choose to give us when you make an enquiry — for example, when you call, message us on WhatsApp, or email. That usually means your name, phone number, and details about the job you'd like quoted (including any photos you send). We use this purely to respond to your enquiry and provide a quote.

Cookies & analytics

This site can use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the website — for example, which pages are most popular. This sets cookies in your browser.

These analytics cookies are not set unless you accept them using the banner shown when you first visit. If you decline, no analytics cookies are placed. The site works exactly the same either way.

To change your choice, you can clear this site's data in your browser settings, then reload the page and choose again.

Sharing your information

We don't sell your information, and we don't share it with anyone except where it's needed to carry out the work you've asked us to quote for or do. Google Analytics data (if you accept) is processed by Google as our analytics provider.

Your rights

You can ask us what information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Just get in touch using the details above and we'll sort it.

Changes

We may update this page from time to time. Any changes will appear here.
